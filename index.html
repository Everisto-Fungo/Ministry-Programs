Ghana Programs

Mountain of the lord camp (MOTL) and give thyself wholly conference (GTW) are hosted by Bishop Dag Heward-Mills the founder of Light house Church and First Love Church. Both programs are to be held at the Aburi Mountains in Ghana. Both Programs were attended by My Fam members in 2019 and this year only promises to be better than the former.

Mountain of the Lord Camp and Give Thyself Wholly Conference which are hosted by Bishop Dag Heward-Mills are life changing programs that inspire one to win for God. Held at the Aburi Mountains in Ghana, the two programmes, as witnessed by the My Fam 2019 delegates, are a must attend for anyone who desires to win the world for Christ. This year of 2020, the two programmes promise to be great and more exciting as more My Fam delegates are expected to attend and experience the life changing and exciting moments provided at MOTL and GTW.
